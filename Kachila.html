Kachila

Kachila is a Newar dish. This traditional and classic Newari dish is basically chopped raw meat mixed with spices. That is, taking into account the traditional cooking method of this dish. Today, this traditionally cooked dish has become a fairly unusual delicacy.The word "Kachila" is derived from the two words "kachyu" and "laa". "Kachu" means raw or fresh, and "la" means meat. Kachira is one of the best Newar dishes you have to try in your life. Click is like a Newar-style steak tartare. Kachira is often eaten as a snack at Newar happy events and festivals . Kachira (raw meat of Newar) Buffalo surimi marinated in black soybean oil with fresh herbs, spices and vegetables

Ingredients

200 grams of minced meat 20 grams fenugreek seed
20 milileter mustard oil
50 grams chopped spring garlic
5 grams coriander leaves
1 dry red chili
Salt to taste
Chili powder to taste

To prepare the click, simply chop the meat into small pieces and add spices. That's all you have to do. You don't have to wait for the food to cool before you eat it. The only thing that takes time to prepare this dish is to heat the pan and chop the meat for Tadoka. This doesn't take much time if you have a powerful grinder.
