Chhon Kwa

Chhon Kwa is one of the most popular and unique Newar dishes. Chhon means bamboo shoots and Kwa means hot soup or stew. This dish is known as Aloo Tama in Nepal and also known as Newar Curry Stew in the Newar community. It is made from potatoes, bamboo shoots (fermented bamboo shoots), and black-eyed beans. Soup is mainly characterized by sourness and spiciness. Since this dish is a gluten-free food, it is an alternative source to a gluten-free diet. It is offered as one of the important side dishes of SamayBajiSet.

Ingredients

3-4 medium-sized potatoes
1 cup fermented bamboo shoots
1 cup black-eyed beans (bodi)
2 medium size tomatoes
2-3 fresh green/red chilies
1 1/2 tbs cumin and coriander powder mix
1 tsp red chilli powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1 tbs ginger paste
1 tbs garlic paste
1 medium size onion
2-3 tbs vegetable oil
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1/2 fenugreek seeds
Salt
